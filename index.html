Our Story

Where Precision
Meets Character

At Ory's Barbershop, we believe a great cut is more than technique — it's a conversation, a ritual, a statement. Founded by Ory and rooted in the vibrant Kreis 5 neighbourhood of Zürich, we blend old-school craft with modern sensibility.

Every client who walks through our door receives the same thing: complete attention, expert hands, and a look tailored precisely to them.
